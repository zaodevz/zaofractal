## Most relevant peers for ZAO

### 1. Eden Fractal - ecosystem hub

- **Why relevant:** Same Superchain, same tech stack (ORDAO + Hats Protocol). Direct collaboration possible.
- **Difference:** General governance vs ZAO's music focus.
- **What to learn:** Epoch 2 deployment patterns, Season structure, on-chain proposal flow.

### 2. Aquadac - process template

- **Why relevant:** Uses Respect Game for peer ranking. Long-running.
- **Difference:** Personal development theme, not creative/music.
- **What to learn:** 12-week seasonal cadence, theme rotation, sustained-participation playbook.

### 3. Roy Fractal - scale proof

- **Why relevant:** Largest fractal by membership (700+). Proves Respect Game scales beyond 50 members.
- **Difference:** EOS-based, governance-focused, not creative.
- **What to learn:** Infrastructure choices and facilitation patterns when membership grows past 100.

## What does not yet exist (greenfield for ZAO)

No existing fractal focuses on:

- Music creation + curation
- Artist reputation + governance
- Creative work peer ranking
- Audio playback in breakout rooms

Related creative DAOs (but non-fractal):

- **Friends With Benefits (FWB)** - token-gated social club, traditional governance
- **SongCamp** - music collective, shared revenue, no peer voting
- **Botto** - AI art DAO, voting on AI-generated art, no Respect Game

There is no direct competition for "music + fractal governance." ZAO holds that position alone.
