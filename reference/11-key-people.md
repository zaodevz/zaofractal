# 11 - Key People

The fractal-governance lineage is built by a small group of people who all know each other. Understanding who they are makes the rest of the lineage make sense.

## Daniel Larimer - philosophical founder

- Creator of **BitShares (2014)** - the first decentralized exchange (DeFi pioneer).
- Creator of **Steem/Hive (2016)** - the first blockchain-based social media with token rewards.
- Creator of **EOS (2018)** - EOSIO smart contract platform, $4B+ market cap at launch.
- Author of **"More Equal Animals: The Subtle Art of True Democracy"** (Feb 20, 2021) - the book that defined fractal democracy.
- Co-author of the **Fractally white paper** (Feb 22, 2022) and the **Addendum 1** scoring revision.
- Founded **Eden on EOS** (April 2021), the first practical fractal community.

After three platform launches, Larimer shifted to governance theory. He is the philosophical root of every fractal community alive today, even though he no longer runs day-to-day operations on any of them. Fractally itself went dormant; the live communities (Eden, Optimism, ZAO, Roy, etc.) carried the idea forward.

## Dan SingJoy - founder, musician, governance innovator

The bridge figure. SingJoy is the link between Larimer's theory and the live fractal communities, and the link between fractal governance and music.

- Placed **3rd in Genesis Fractal** (2022, ~130 participants).
- Founded **Eden Fractal** (May 2022).
- Founded **Optimism Fractal** (Oct 2023, paused Jan 2026).
- Co-founded **Optimystics**, the dev studio behind ORDAO/OREC/frapps/Fractalgram.
- Founded **Eden Creators** and **Eden Town Hall**.
- 1,600 Respect points - the highest contributor to Optimism Fractal.
- Hosts **Creator Talk**, **Fractal Apple** (a cinematic show combining music and public goods), and **Dan's Party** (YouTube music).
- Created **"Fractal DJ"** - a variant of the Respect Game for ranking music selections.

Critical for ZAO: SingJoy is fundamentally a **musician-turned-governance-innovator**. His personal brand is music + governance. He and Zaal are natural allies. His philosophy: *"Well-designed social games are the key to leveling up our communities, societies, and civilization."*

Find him: [@DanSingjoy](https://warpcast.com/dansingjoy) on Farcaster, on X, on GitHub (26 repos).

## Tadas Vaitiekunas (`sim31`) - lead developer

The technical core of every modern fractal community.

- Co-founded **Optimystics**.
- Built **ORDAO**, **OREC**, **Fractalgram** - all the core technical infrastructure.
- 850 Respect points (Optimism Fractal).
- GitHub: [`sim31`](https://github.com/sim31) - the upstream `ordao` repo lives at `sim31/ordao` with 254+ commits, last push April 2, 2026.
- Personally deployed **`zao.frapps.xyz`** for ZAO Fractal.
- Released the **original Fractalgram** (Feb 2023) - the Telegram Web A fork that automates Respect Game sessions.
- Relationship with Zaal: described as "very friendly, active daily/weekly discussions."

If something on-chain breaks at any fractal community, Tadas is the person who fixes it.

## Why the people matter for ZAO

ZAO is not a standalone DAO. It is a chapter in a 5-year governance movement built by a small group of people who all know each other. Larimer wrote the theory. SingJoy built the practical communities. Tadas built the software. Zaal applied it to music and is now the longest-running operator of a weekly fractal anywhere.

Any external pitch (grants, partnerships, press) is stronger when it tells this lineage rather than presenting ZAO as a one-off DAO.
