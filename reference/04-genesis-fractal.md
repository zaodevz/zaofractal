# 04 - Genesis Fractal (2022)

## What it was

A **30-week experimental fractal community** that ran in 2022 to refine the Fractally Respect Game between Eden on EOS (election-based) and Eden Fractal proper (weekly-game-based).

Genesis Fractal is the missing link. Eden on EOS proved a fractal community could *exist*. Genesis Fractal proved the *weekly Respect Game* worked as a complement to elections. Without Genesis, Eden Fractal would not have launched in the form it did, and Eden Fractal is the parent of every fractal alive today.

## What it tested

Quarterly elections worked but were slow. Genesis Fractal asked: what if the small-group ranking ritual happens *every week*, and the output is a soulbound reputation token rather than an elected delegate?

The experiment found:

- People kept coming back. Weekly cadence created momentum and community identity that quarterly elections never did.
- Peer ranking on a Fibonacci curve felt fair to participants. Top earners were not resented; bottom-ranked members did not quit.
- 6-person groups produced rich enough discussion to differentiate contributions; smaller groups felt thin, larger groups felt rushed.
- A 30-week run accumulated a meaningful Respect ledger that could - in theory - feed into governance weight downstream.

## Why this matters for ZAO

The weekly cadence that ZAO Fractal has now run for **100+ continuous weeks** was first proven viable by Genesis Fractal in 2022. ZAO did not invent the Monday-night ritual. ZAO inherited it from Fractally, via Genesis Fractal, via Eden Fractal.

What ZAO contributed is **longevity** (no other fractal has run weekly this long without pause), **focus** (the first music-and-culture fractal), and **embedding** (the first fractal living inside a full social client - ZAO OS on Farcaster).

Dan SingJoy - who placed 3rd in Genesis Fractal - went on to found Eden Fractal in May 2022 and later Optimism Fractal in October 2023. He remains the bridge figure between Larimer's theory, the live communities, and ZAO. See [11-key-people.md](11-key-people.md).

## Status today

Concluded. Genesis Fractal was always a 30-week experiment, not a permanent community. Its participants - led by Dan SingJoy - rolled into Eden Fractal in May 2022, which is the same community-thread that now runs as Eden Fractal Epoch 2 on Base. See [05-eden-fractal.md](05-eden-fractal.md).
