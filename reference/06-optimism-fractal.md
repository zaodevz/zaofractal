# 06 - Optimism Fractal (October 2023 - January 2026) - PAUSED

## What it was

The fractal that **brought the model from EOS to Ethereum**. Launched October 2023 by Dan SingJoy and the Optimystics team on **OP Mainnet**, specifically to bring fractal governance into the Optimism ecosystem.

Optimism Fractal proved that ORDAO/OREC tooling could run on Ethereum L2s under real conditions. It also funded its own work via the Optimism Grants Council and ran the largest body of on-chain fractal proposals to date.

## The tripartite governance model

Optimism Fractal pioneered a three-branch structure inside a fractal community:

- **Judicial branch** - the Respect Game itself (peer evaluation)
- **Legislative branch** - the **Sages Council** (up to 6 highest-Respect members, managed via Hats Protocol, elected from breakout rooms)
- **Executive branch** - ORDAO/OREC automated on-chain execution

Season 5 (Nov 2024) was the milestone where the community council formally approved ORDAO as the executive layer. This was the first production deployment of ORDAO on Ethereum mainnet and the testbed for everything Eden later adopted in Epoch 2.

## The grant

The Optimism Grants Council (Season 6) awarded Optimism Fractal a research grant titled *"Respect Game: Research into Democratic Fund Distribution."* This was formal recognition by the Optimism Foundation that fractal governance was a credible democratic fund-allocation model for the OP Collective.

## The pause

In **January 2026**, the Optimism Fractal Council voted to pause indefinitely and consolidate into Eden Fractal. The official announcement on optimismfractal.com states:

> *"After two incredible years of pioneering the Respect Game and fractal governance on the Superchain, the Optimism Fractal Council has approved an indefinite pause to consolidate our efforts on Eden Fractal."*

## What the pause means for ZAO

Two things changed the moment Optimism Fractal paused:

1. **ZAO Fractal became the only active fractal on Optimism.**
2. **ZAO Fractal became one of only two active fractals on the entire Ethereum Superchain** (the other being Eden Fractal on Base).

This is a strategic position - for grants, partnerships, ecosystem visibility, and as the reference music-governance implementation that any future Optimism fractal would naturally reference.

## Why it paused

Two factors, both publicly documented:

- **Participation gravity.** A community-of-communities benefits from concentration. Two general-purpose fractals splitting attention on the Superchain was not sustainable; one focused effort (Eden) was.
- **Tooling consolidation.** ORDAO and frapps need a single canonical deployment to evolve quickly. Two parallel production deployments on the Superchain doubled the maintenance load on a small dev team without doubling the learning.

The pause is not a verdict on fractal governance - Eden continues, ZAO continues, Roy Fractal continues at 700+ members on EOS. It is a verdict on running two parallel general-purpose fractals on the same ecosystem.
