## Key facts surfaced

**EOS chain (Sub 1):**
- EOS ICO June 2017-June 2018, raised $4B+
- Peak price $22.89 April 2018, now $0.40 (-99.6%)
- Block.one SEC settlement Sept 30 2019, $24M fine
- Larimer departure Dec 31, 2020
- Voice social network launched July 2020, shut down April 2021
- Antelope rebrand Sept 21 2022 (hard fork)
- ENF (EOS Network Foundation) formed Aug 25, 2021

**Fractally protocol (Sub 1):**
- Larimer "More Equal Animals" Feb 20, 2021
- Fractally announcement Jan 28, 2022
- White paper Feb 22, 2022
- Addendum 1 scoring revision mid-2022
- Zero contracts deployed (the protocol shipped a spec but no production)
- Dormant since ~2023
- Survived: the IDEA + the WHITE PAPER + the people who carried it forward

**Eden Fractal (Sub 2):**
- Eden on EOS first election Oct 9 2021, 13:00 UTC, 182 registrants
- 9 election cycles, ~$1.5M distributed
- Genesis Fractal: 30 weeks, 130 participants, ~37 weekly avg
- Eden Fractal Epoch 1 (EOS): 77 contributors, 100+ events
- **Epoch 1.5 false start announced Aug 14, 2024**, took 10-month engineering rebuild
- Epoch 2 launch June 5, 2025 = Event #121 (3-year anniversary)
- Currently: 130+ events, Season 12, ~40 active, bi-weekly Thursdays 17:00 UTC

**Optimism Fractal (Sub 3):**
- Launched Oct 2023 by Dan SingJoy + Optimystics
- Tripartite governance: Judicial (Respect Game), Legislative (Sages Council via Hats Protocol), Executive (ORDAO/OREC)
- 72 bi-weekly events, ~65 Respect holders
- ORDAO formally approved Season 5 Nov 2024 - first production deployment of ORDAO on any Ethereum mainnet
- Optimism Grants Council Season 6 grant: "Respect Game: Research into Democratic Fund Distribution"
- **Paused indefinitely Jan 2026** to consolidate into Eden
- 5 ranked causes: Eden Epoch 2 resource demand (strong), strategic consolidation (strong), volunteer burnout (moderate), participation plateau (weak), grant window (weak)
- Post-pause: Respect tokens frozen on Optimism, no migration path announced
- Result: ZAO Fractal became the only active fractal on Optimism

**Optimystics tools (Sub 4):**
- 10 primary tools + 3 bonus tools cataloged
- 16 GitHub repos in the Optimystics org
- Tadas Vaitiekunas (sim31) last commit May 20, 2026 - actively maintained
- ZAO verdicts: USE (1) / PILOT (5) / IGNORE (3) / ABANDON (1)
- USE: ORDAO/OREC (core infra)
- PILOT: Respect.Games (async track), Cignals (music ranking), Cagendas (agenda), Respect Trees (UI), RetroPolls
- IGNORE: Fractalgram (Telegram-only - ZAO is Discord-first), OPTOPICS, Firmament (paused)
- ABANDON: Fractal Circles (founder unreachable - route to Frapp-GH instead)
