# 08 - Eden Creators Garden: Full Inventory

> **Goal:** Catalog every project, tool, game, and initiative listed on the [Eden Creators Garden](https://edencreators.com/garden) - the meta-blog Dan SingJoy + the Eden Fractal community maintain as a "fractal, iterative blog." 30+ entries. Cross-link with existing ZAOfractal coverage and surface what is genuinely new vs already covered.

## What the Garden is

A landing page at edencreators.com/garden that links to Eden Creators' draft articles. SingJoy's framing: "more lively, iterative, and fractal" than a blog. Each linked article is a work-in-progress that gets revisited and updated over time.

This is the closest thing the wider Eden + Optimystics ecosystem has to a master index. Reading it cold reveals tools and initiatives that did not appear in our prior coverage of Optimystics' GitHub org (which surfaces code) or optimystics.io/communities (which surfaces fractals). The Garden surfaces the EXPERIMENTS - half-formed, conceptual, sometimes paused.

## Status legend

For each entry below:
- **LIVE** - actively maintained, has working surface
- **DRAFT** - article exists, project status unknown or experimental
- **DORMANT** - was active, no recent updates
- **CONCEPT** - idea-stage only

When the Garden page itself describes something as "draft", we mark it DRAFT here.

## What this reveals about the Optimystics + Eden ecosystem

### A larger tool surface than our optimystics-tools-survey captured

`research/fractal-deep/04-optimystics-tools-survey.md` covers 10 tools + 3 bonus. The Garden surfaces another 12+ that are largely EXPERIMENTAL / CONCEPT-stage, sitting between an idea and a shipped tool. These were not visible from the Optimystics GitHub org (which is code) or from optimystics.io (which only highlights shipped products). The Garden is where the half-formed lives.

### The "meeting meta-game" pattern

EFBS (Eden Fractal Brainstorming Sessions) is a recurring meta-meeting about how to improve the recurring meetings. ZAO does not run an explicit equivalent. The closest ZAO has: ad-hoc retros and Tanja's documentation-gap call (Doc 675). A formal biweekly meta-fractal would be a direct steal-from-Eden move. See Doc 703 Key Decision #4 ("non-technical onboarding") - EFBS-style sessions could fill that gap.

### The Consortium / ORDAO duality

Both Consortium (voting app, Eden Creators framing) and ORDAO (executive contract, Optimystics framing) exist in the Eden ecosystem. ORDAO is what ZAO uses. Consortium appears to be its precursor or its complementary opinion-signaling layer. Worth deeper investigation if ZAO ever needs a non-Respect-weighted opinion poll surface beyond Snapshot.

### Pomelo, ARC, Renaissance: external alliances

The Garden is open about EOS-world adjacencies the modern Eden Fractal still works with - Pomelo crowdfunding (EOS-native), ARC (Alliance for Responsible Citizenship - mainstream civics nonprofit), and the Renaissance framing (EOS + HitRecord + Patreon). These are not fractal-internal; they are how Eden talks to the wider world. ZAO has analogous outward-facing partnerships (ZABAL Games, COC Concertz, FISHBOWLZ) but the Garden is the canonical place Eden surfaces them. Worth noting how Eden's outreach reads - it is unapologetically EOS-historical even in 2026.

### DAMS as a video-collaboration primitive

DAMS (Decentralized Autonomous Movie Studios) reads like a community-video-production model. ZAO's video output (BCZ YapZ, WaveWarZ artist content, ZAOMusicBot demos) does not currently use a DAMS-style framework. If video production at ZAO needs to formalize attribution + distributed-budget + collective-direction, DAMS is the prior-art reference.
