## How to use

When drafting a whitepaper chapter, open the corresponding sub-doc and pull:

- The argument structure (each sub-doc opens with Key Findings, then walks through them)
- Specific numbers (every claim has a date, percentage, or contract address)
- Direct quotations (every primary source quote is attributed)
- The "For the Whitepaper" sections (where present, they distill the source material into chapter-ready paragraphs)
